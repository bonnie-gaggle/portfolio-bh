Voiceover

I enjoy using my voice to bring stories to life, especially juvenile and YA fiction or eLearning. I have a neutral American sound with clear articulation. My voice has a bright tone and mid to high pitch. I have been volunteering with Learning Ally, mainly narrating the Fablehaven series. With my in-home studio, I can turn around work quickly, including basic de-clicking and breath reduction. If you need an engaging narrator for audiobooks, eLearning, or anything in between, feel free to contact me!

You can also check out a couple of samples on the very first web page I built.

Teaching

I taught science for 10 years, 8 of which was at the K-12 level. My favorite part of the job was figuring out how to break down complex topics into manageable pieces and find ways to make things engaging. I also enjoyed creating systems (such as utilizing spreadsheets) to streamline my teaching and evaluation and sharing those tools with others. While I'm excited to move on to a new chapter of life, I am grateful for the many soft skills I honed while teaching, such as collaboration, leadership, and problem-solving.
